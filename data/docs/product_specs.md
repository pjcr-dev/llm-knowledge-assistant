# HyperGizmo 3000 - Specifications

## Hardware
- Dimensions: 12cm x 8cm x 2cm
- Weight: 250g
- Processor: Quad-core 2.5GHz
- Memory: 8GB RAM
- Storage: 256GB SSD

## Connectivity
- Wi-Fi 6
- Bluetooth 5.2
- USB-C, HDMI, and Ethernet ports

## Battery
- Capacity: 5000mAh
- Runtime: Up to 10 hours
- Charging: Fast-charge 0–80% in 45 minutes

## Environmental
- Operating Temperature: 0°C to 40°C
- Storage Temperature: -20°C to 60°C
